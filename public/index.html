How to Play CodePiece

Teams: P1 & P3 (Team 1) vs P2 & P4 (Team 2). Seated clockwise.

Objective: Win 7 tricks in a game. Win the most games to win the match.

Setup: 52 cards dealt equally (13 each). Trump chooser rotates each game.

"I am Side": If you have no cards from a suit, all players are told before play begins.

Trump: The trump chooser picks the trump suit. Any trump card beats any non-trump card. Lowest trump (2) beats highest non-trump (Ace).

Playing a trick: You must follow the led suit if you have it. If you don't, you may play any card — including trump.

Card values: A > K > Q > J > 10 > 9 > ... > 2

Match: 4 games per match. Team with the most game wins wins the match.
